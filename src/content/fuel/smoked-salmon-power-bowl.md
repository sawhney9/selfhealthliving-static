	<div class="wprm-fallback-recipe-ingredients">
		<ul><li>1 cup cooked sushi rice or short-grain rice</li><li>1 tbsp rice vinegar (For the Rice)</li><li>8 oz Smoked Salmon</li><li>1 Ripe Avocado</li><li>1 tbsp sesame seeds</li><li>1/2 cup Sliced cucumber (Thinly sliced into half moons)</li><li>1-2 Roasted Seaweed (Crushed or sliced)</li><li>1 Sliced radish ((adds color and tang))</li><li>2 tbsp Chopped green onions</li><li>1 tbsp low sodium soy sauce or tamari</li><li>1-2 tsp Chili Oil (Depending how spicy you like it) (I feel the right chili oil really makes a difference and so I prefer momofuku or from a good local Asian store.)</li><li>2 tbsp Mayo ((I like Japanese mayo, but any will do))</li><li>1 tsp Water (Helps to mix the sauce)</li></ul>	</div>
	<div class="wprm-fallback-recipe-instructions">
		<ol><li><p><strong>Base Prep (Your Foundation)</strong></p><p><strong>If using traditional rice:</strong> If your rice isn&#039;t already cooked, get that going! Once it&#039;s ready and still warm, gently fold in the rice vinegar. This gives it that classic sushi rice tang and helps keep it from clumping.</p><p><strong>Low Carb Swaps</strong></p><p>2 cups cooked cauliflower rice (steamed, sautéed, or microwaved, If you want a &quot;rice-like&quot; experience, you can even toss it with a tiny splash of rice vinegar and a pinch of salt.)</p><p>or @ cups cooked quinoa ( for a protein packed, gluten free grain option)</p></li><li><p><strong>Dressing Magic: </strong>In a small bowl, whisk together the low-sodium soy sauce, chili oil, mayo, and water until you have a smooth, luscious, and beautifully orange dressing. Give it a taste – need more kick? Add a little more chili oil!</p></li><li><p><strong>Assemble Your Masterpiece:</strong></p><p>- Grab your favorite bowls (the prettier, the better, right?).</p><p>- Spoon your chosen base (rice, cauliflower-rice, quinoa) evenly into the bottom of the bowls.</p><p>- Divide the thinly sliced smoked salmon evenly between the bowls.</p><p>- Divide and nestle in the sliced avocado and cucumber into each bowl.</p><p><strong></strong></p></li><li><p><strong>Garnish generously: </strong>Sprinkle with sesame seeds, green onions, and crushed or thinly sliced roasted seaweed. </p></li><li><p><strong>Drizzle and enjoy: </strong>Finish with the spicy mayo dressing or serve it on the side.</p></li></ol>	</div>

<p data-pm-slice="1 1 []"><span style="font-weight: 400">This </span><b>Smoked Salmon Poke-Style Power Bowl</b><span style="font-weight: 400"> is a fun, vibrant way to eat clean without sacrificing taste. Inspired by Hawaiian poke bowls, this version is a no-cook, weeknight-friendly dinner that brings together savory smoked salmon, buttery avocado, and fresh veggies — all beautifully arranged over a bed of sushi rice.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">Imagine the gentle saltiness of smoked salmon, the creamy bite of ripe avocado, and the refreshing crunch of thin-sliced radishes and cucumber—all drizzled with a spicy mayo. It's like your favorite sushi roll deconstructed into a colorful, customizable bowl!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">It’s perfect for anyone living the self-health lifestyle: rich in omega-3s, heart-healthy fats, fiber, and flavor. And the best part? You can make it in under 20 minutes with zero cooking required (yea you read that right, other than some sushi rice). Whether you're winding down from work or hosting a casual dinner, this bowl delivers comfort and nutrition in every bite.</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><b>HIGH PROTEIN!</b><span style="font-weight: 400"> Smoked salmon is a fantastic source of lean protein, which is essential for muscle repair, feeling full and satisfied, and keeping those energy levels steady throughout the day. </span><b>And with the quinoa option, you're doubling down on that protein, as quinoa is a complete protein source!</b><span style="font-weight: 400"> Say goodbye to that 3 PM slump!</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><b>HEALTHY FATS GALORE!</b><span style="font-weight: 400"> The smoked salmon and avocado are packed with healthy monounsaturated and omega-3 fatty acids. These are brain-boosting, heart-healthy fats that help reduce inflammation and keep your skin glowing.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><b>FIBER-PACKED! </b><span style="font-weight: 400">Whether you choose the traditional rice, cauliflower rice, quinoa, or greens, you're getting a good dose of fiber from the veggies and base. Fiber is crucial for digestive health and blood sugar control.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><b>LOW-CARB &amp; NO-RICE FLEXIBILITY!</b><span style="font-weight: 400"> This is where the magic happens!</span>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><b>Cauliflower Rice:</b><span style="font-weight: 400"> An incredible way to enjoy the "poke bowl" experience without the carb load. It soaks up the dressing beautifully and adds a subtle texture.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><b>Quinoa:</b><span style="font-weight: 400"> A fantastic gluten-free grain alternative that's packed with protein and fiber, making it super satisfying and nutritious without the higher carb count of traditional rice.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><b>Mixed Greens/Spinach:</b><span style="font-weight: 400"> For an even lighter, greens-based bowl that's packed with vitamins and minerals. Perfect if you're really watching those macros!</span></li>
</ul>
</li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><b>FLAVOR EXPLOSION!</b><span style="font-weight: 400"> Who says healthy has to be bland? That spicy mayo and soy dressing hits all the savory, umami, and spicy notes, making every mouthful an adventure. The combination of creamy avocado, crisp cucumber, and silky salmon is just divine.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><b>QUICK &amp; EASY:</b><span style="font-weight: 400"> This bowl comes together in minutes, especially if you have your base ready. Perfect for a busy weeknight dinner or a power-packed work-from-home lunch.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><b>CUSTOMIZATION IS KEY:</b><span style="font-weight: 400"> This is YOUR bowl!</span>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><b>Veggies:</b><span style="font-weight: 400"> Add shredded carrots, edamame, or even thinly sliced bell pepper strips for more color and nutrients.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><b>Heat Level:</b><span style="font-weight: 400"> Adjust the chili oil to your personal preference. A little goes a long way, but sometimes you just need that extra fire!</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400"><b>Extra Crunch:</b><span style="font-weight: 400"> Want more texture? Add some toasted slivered almonds or even a sprinkle of crispy fried garlic.</span></li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>

Give them a whirl and let me know how you customize yours! What other "power bowls" should we transform next? I'm always open to ideas!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400">This bowl is easily </span><b>gluten-free</b><span style="font-weight: 400"> (with tamari) and adaptable for low-carb diets (swap rice for greens or cauliflower rice).</span></p>
