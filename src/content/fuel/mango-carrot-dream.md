<p><span style="font-weight: 400">   – Studies: Consumption of strawberries is linked to improved heart health and reduced </span><a href="https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC7828105/"><span style="font-weight: 400">oxidative stress</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400">.</span></p>
