  instructions:
    - "<strong>Step 1: Make the Batter </strong>In a mixing bowl, combine chickpea flour, chopped onions, peppers, grated carrots, green chili, cumin seeds, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and ajwain. Add salt to taste.Slowly add water and whisk to form a smooth, lump-free batter. The batter should be of pouring consistency, neither too thick nor too runny."
    - "<strong>Step 2:</strong> <strong>Heat the Pan</strong>Heat a pan or griddle on medium heat and lightly grease it with oil or ghee."
    - "<strong>Step 3:</strong> <strong>Pour the Batter</strong>Pour a ladleful of batter onto the pan and spread it out gently in a circular motion to make it as thin as possible, this way it will cook faster."
    - "<strong>Step 4: Cook the Pancake</strong>Cook the chilla for 2-3 minutes on one side until the edges start lifting and the surface appears cooked.
